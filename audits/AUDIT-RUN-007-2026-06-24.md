## 3. Findings

### F06 — No dedicated module for investment-research / market-analysis *content* — **S2 (High)**
- **Location:** Stage C module set (Group 1 A–W, Group 2 X–AE, Group 3 AF–AP).
- **Evidence:** The financial modules target neighbouring objects, not the research artifact: **AF** audits an issuer's *internal control over financial reporting* (SOX/ICFR); **AM** audits *quantitative models* (model risk, SR 11-7); **AK** audits *sustainability disclosures*. Generic modules give partial cover — **J** (statistical validity/data quality), **K** (AI generation), **O** (document quality), **Q** (compliance mapping), **B1–B4** (correctness/completeness). But **nothing names the research-conduct regimes or encodes the equity-research analytical checklist** (reasonable basis, multiple-method valuation with explicit assumptions + sensitivity, fact-vs-opinion separation, conflicts disclosure & analyst certification, performance-claim substantiation, AI-use disclosure). A market report or stock-movement insight therefore has no home module.
- **Impact:** High (an entire requested artifact class is uncovered) · **Likelihood:** High (this is a common artifact) · **Status:** Resolved · **Resolved by:** R06.

## 4. Validated standards (Verification ledger — sourced 2026-06-24)
Industry-standard frameworks for auditing this content, by what they govern:

- **Research conduct, conflicts & disclosure:** FINRA **Rule 2241** (equity) / **2242** (debt) — reasonable basis, research/banking separation, conflict disclosure; SEC **Regulation AC** (17 CFR §242.500–505) — analyst certification; EU **MAR Article 20 + Commission Delegated Reg (EU) 2016/958** — objective presentation & conflict disclosure (applies since 3 Jul 2016); **SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations 2014**, last amended **16 Dec 2024** + 2025 guidelines (registration, MITC, records, model-portfolio, **AI-use disclosure**); **IOSCO** Statement of Principles for Addressing Sell-Side Securities Analyst Conflicts of Interest (2003).
- **Research-payment context (changing in 2026):** **EU Listing Act** (in force 4 Dec 2024) reverses MiFID II research *unbundling*; EU effect ~mid-2026 (UK live since Aug 2024) — affects research-payment disclosures.
- **Analytical rigor / reasonable basis:** **CFA Institute** Standard **V(A)** Diligence & Reasonable Basis (guidance updated Apr 2024) and **V(B)** Communication with Clients; equity-research best practice = ≥2 triangulated valuation methods (DCF + comparables/precedents), explicit assumptions, sensitivity/scenario stress-tests (CFA flags projection quality as the #1 analytical-error source).
- **Performance / track-record claims:** **GIPS 2020** (Firms / Asset Owners / Verifiers) + independent verification.
- **Data & methodology integrity:** look-ahead / survivorship / selection bias, backtest overfitting & data-snooping, out-of-sample evidence; ISO/IEC 25012 data quality + source/citation integrity (Module J); EU Benchmarks Regulation if an index is built.
- **AI-generated research:** **IOSCO — AI in Capital Markets** Consultation Report (Mar 2025, PD788) → **Supervisory Toolkit for AI Use in Capital Markets** (May 2026, FR/02/2026, PD823); **NIST AI RMF** + GenAI Profile; SEBI AI-use disclosure.
- **Assurance framing:** **ISAE 3000** (assurance over non-financial / other information), reasonable vs limited.
